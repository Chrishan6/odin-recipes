Truffle Pasta

Ingredients

  • 35g of black truffles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Spaghetti, but tagliolini, linguine, and so on will be fine as well.

Instructions

1. Cook the pasta al dente. You will need some cooking water for the sauce, so once the spaghetti is almost cooked, you should have all the sauce ingredients ready for the pan.

2. Making the truffle pasta sauce only takes minutes. Grate the Parmesan and set it aside. Cube the butter and set it aside. Grate about ⅔ of the truffles and set them aside. Melt the in a large heavy-bottomed skillet on medium-high heat. Add about one ladleful of pasta water (more or less 75 ml/ 2.5 fl. oz/ ⅓ cups) to the butter. Turn the heat down to low and stir gently until the mixture emulsifies. Add the grated Parmesan

3. Once the sauce is smooth (it will take less than a minute), add the grated truffles and another splash of the cooking water. Stir gently, turn off the heat, and let the sauce infuse for a minute or so while you drain the spaghetti.

4. Add the noodles to the skillet and stir gently to combine

Why Truffle Pasta?

Truffle pasta offers a luxurious and unforgettable culinary experience, combining the earthy and aromatic flavors of truffles with creamy pasta. The unique and intense aroma of truffles elevates the dish to a gourmet level, making it a must-try for food enthusiasts. Each bite is a harmonious blend of rich, savory, and umami flavors, creating a decadent treat that lingers on the palate, making truffle pasta an irresistible indulgence for those seeking a truly memorable dining experience.
